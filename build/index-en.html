Professional dental care for the whole family.

Regular check-ups, dental and gum care are essential for maintaining good oral health. We offer a full range of preventive services and dental treatment. Our "for patients" page contains useful information on what to expect during your first visit.

We provide the following services:

Preventive Care

Regular check-ups and fluoride treatments to prevent tooth decay and gum disease.

Orthopaedic dentistry

Zirconia crowns, IPS e.Max, bridges, removable dentures, restoration of damaged teeth.

Surgical dentistry

Wisdom teeth extraction and other surgical procedures.

Aesthetic dentistry

Comprehensive professional hygiene, whitening, ceramic veneers, and other procedures to improve the aesthetic appearance of teeth.

Implantation

Dental implants are a popular and effective way to restore lost teeth.
